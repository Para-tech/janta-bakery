Our Journey

From a small kitchen to your hearts

Janta Bakery was started in 1998 by Ramesh and Sunita Sharma in a small room in the heart of our town. With just one oven and a dream, they began baking bread for their neighbours every morning.

Word spread fast. People loved the warm, fresh smell of bread and the honest taste of homemade cakes. Over the years, we grew — but we never changed our recipe: simple ingredients, lots of care, and a big heart.

Today, Janta Bakery is a beloved name in the community. We still wake up at 4 AM every day to make sure your morning starts with something special. Because for us, baking is not just a business — it is our passion.
